my education.

When I entered high school in the fall of 2014, I was in the gifted program at McKinley Senior High School. During my sophomore year, I decided that I wanted to be more challenged by my classes, so I transferred to Lee Magnet High School in the fall of 2016 for my junior year.

At McKinley, I was a part of Key Club and Mu Alpha Theta (with a brief stint in Youth Legislature, before deciding that civics wasn't for me). Being in the gifted program, my core classes were all gifted (equivalent to honors), with the exception of my sophomore year civics/AP U.S. Gov. class. At Lee, I'm taking all AP core classes and am founder + president of Key Club and Mu Captain of Mu Alpha Theta. Next year, I will be vice president of Mu Alpha Theta and will remain as president of Key Club. Throughout my high school career, I have maintained a 4.5 average GPA.
